Food is any substance consumed by an organism for nutritional support.
Food usually consists of plant, animal, or fungal origin and contains
essential nutrients such as carbohydrates, fats, proteins, vitamins, 
or minerals. The substance is ingested by an organism and assimilated 
by the organism's cells to provide energy, maintain life, encourage physical 
requirements, and support growth. Different species of animals have different   feeding behaviours that satisfy the needs of their metabolisms and have 
evolved to fill specific ecological niches within specific geographical 
contexts.
